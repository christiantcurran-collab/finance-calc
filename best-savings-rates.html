What to Look For in a Savings Account

  • AER (Annual Equivalent Rate) - Shows what you'll earn over a year including compound interest
  • Access Terms - Easy access, notice periods, or fixed terms affect when you can withdraw
  • FSCS Protection - Ensure savings are protected up to £85,000 per institution
  • Minimum/Maximum Balance - Check deposit requirements and limits
  • Bonus Rates - Some accounts offer introductory bonuses that expire after a set period
  • Tax Treatment - Consider your Personal Savings Allowance or use a Cash ISA for tax-free interest
