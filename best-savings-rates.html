What to Look For in a Savings Account

  • Interest Rate (AER) - Annual Equivalent Rate shows what you'll earn over a year including compound interest
  • Access Terms - Consider how quickly you need access to your money
  • FSCS Protection - Ensure your savings are protected up to £85,000 per institution
  • Minimum/Maximum Balance - Check deposit requirements and limits
  • Withdrawal Restrictions - Be aware of any penalties or limits on accessing funds
  • Bonus Rates - Some accounts offer introductory bonuses that expire after a set period
